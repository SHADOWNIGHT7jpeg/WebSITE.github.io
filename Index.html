Climate change is a pressing global issue characterized by long-term alterations in temperature, precipitation, and other atmospheric conditions. Human activities, particularly the burning of fossil fuels and deforestation, contribute significantly to the increase in greenhouse gas emissions, intensifying the greenhouse effect and leading to global warming. This phenomenon has far-reaching consequences, including rising sea levels, extreme weather events, and disruptions to ecosystems. Mitigating climate change requires collective efforts, sustainable practices, and international cooperation to reduce carbon emissions and adapt to the changing climate.
